About FocusTime

FocusTime is a productivity tool based on the Pomodoro technique. It helps you manage your time by working in focused intervals, followed by short breaks, to maintain high productivity levels without burnout.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

How the Pomodoro Technique Works

  • Work for 25 minutes (1 Pomodoro Session)
  • Take a 5-minute break after each session
  • After 4 sessions, take a longer break (15-30 minutes)

Benefits of using FocusTime

  • Improves focus and concentration
  • Reduces procrastination
  • Stay on track with your tasks
  • Helps manage distractions
